Marketing campaign

Challenge

Increase brand awareness - the ability of the audience to recognize or remember YOTA's brand when choosing a product or service, as well as before purchasing it. According to company representatives, brand awareness has grown steadily for three to four years, but not as fast as we would like.

Denis Eliseev

Creative Director of Friends Moscow Agency

The challenge of communication is to stand out and get additional media coverage. And we managed to stand out during the maximum noise from all sides, without creating something fancy.

Inspiration

While working on the idea, the campaign developers are focused on the insight that is based on the Yota product - the operator does not impose on the user. No tariffs - everyone chooses services independently, depending on their standard needs. Therefore, the brand and the agencies decided not to hide from users that they are facing Yota ads.

Idea

Friends Moscow (the marketing agency) put forward a hypothesis - if you do not shout to the user in the face about the product, then in contrast he will remember the brand, because there are much-standardized advertising around, constantly offering to buy something.

Growth

  • Spontaneous brand awareness increased by 10%.
  • Advertising knowledge has tripled.
  • The growth of organic traffic to the site has grown by 40%.
  • The growth of orders for SIM-cards on the site has grown by 80%.
  • Increase in offline sales by 17%.
  • Increase in positive and neutral mentions on social networks tenfold.
